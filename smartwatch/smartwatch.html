pTron Pulsefit P261 Bluetooth calling fitness smartwatch comes with a 4.31 cm (1.7 inch) HD touch screen which gives a clearer display of your health data and supports customized watch dial images. This smartwatch is compatible with iOS 9.1/Android 8.0 and above smartphones and tablets. This fitness activity tracker supports 8 exercise modes and many other fitness functions. Connect your fitness watch to the 'pTron Fit+' mobile app and analyze your fitness data in more detail. Check your heart rate via Pulsefit P261 smartwatch, and track your sleep patterns with in-depth breakdowns via the mobile app. Connect this smart watch with your device via a mobile App and get real-time notifications of your calls, texts, and social media messages. Other functions: 250 mAh Battery, 3Hrs Charging, BT Calls via watch, Music & Camera Remote, and many more features.
